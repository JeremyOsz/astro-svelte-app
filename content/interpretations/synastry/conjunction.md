# Synastry Conjunction

orb: 0-8°
nature: Harmonious when planets are compatible, challenging when planets have conflicting energies

### General
Planets in conjunction create powerful soul connections between two people. This aspect represents unity, instant recognition, and deep understanding. The planets work as one, amplifying each other's characteristics in the relationship.

compatibility: harmonious
intensity: strong

### Interpretation

### Romance

### Friendship

### Family
Strong family bond with shared identity. You naturally understand each other's core values and life purposes, creating a supportive family dynamic.

### Business
