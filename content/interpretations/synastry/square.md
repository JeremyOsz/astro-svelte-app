# Synastry Square

orb: 90° ±8°
nature: Challenging, requires effort and growth to harmonize

### General
Planets in square create tension and conflict that requires conscious effort to resolve between two people. This aspect represents challenges, obstacles, and the need for growth through struggle. It often manifests as internal conflicts or external challenges that push both people to develop.
