# Synastry Opposition

orb: 180° ±8°
nature: Challenging but can be harmonized through awareness and integration

### General
Planets in opposition create tension and awareness through contrast between two people. This aspect represents relationships, balance, and the need to integrate opposing forces. It often manifests as complementary dynamics that require conscious effort to harmonize.
